HOW TO BUY

01

Go to https://pancakeswap.finance, connect your wallet and paste the contract. Make sure you have enough BNB to cover the binance smart chain gas fee.

02

Type the amount you want to buy, click on the Settings icon, adjust the slippage to somewhere between 8% and click on Swap. Make sure that you are using Pancake Swap V2.

03

When starting trading you might also need to approve the Miku contract on Pancake Swap by signing a transaction with your wallet.
